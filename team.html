TEMITOPE ONIFADE

FOUNDER

Temitope graduated with LLB degree from Obafemi Awolowo University, Ile-Ife, Nigeria and thereafter proceeded to the Nigerian Law School wherein he obtained his BL. Immediately after his mandatory Youth Service, he joined the law firm of Odoemena A.C & Co. Katsina State, a corporate-commercial law firm with interest in Real Estate practice. While there, he garnered experience in litigation, Commercial and Real Estate practice. He was later appointed as a Senior Legislative Aide at the Ekiti State House of Assembly. He is also an advisor to the Ekiti State House of Assembly advocacy Committee on Female Genital Mutilation till date. Temitope is a lover of books and New Age music.

AKINTUNDE IBIRONKE

PARTNER

Akintunde had his LLB from Obafemi Awolowo University, Ile-Ife, Nigeria, and joined Bamidele & Co. after obtaining his BL from the Nigerian Law School. Akintunde's interest cuts across fields of law, but he maintains a dogged interest in commercial litigation.
